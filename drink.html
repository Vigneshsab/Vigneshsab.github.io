About

WOW Pizza has been in existence for over 50 years. With around 13,000 restaurants in 130 countries, we are the pizza specialist. WOW Pizza has been in Germany since 1983. In over 60 restaurants and Express Stores, we bake the best pizza for you every day. With the quality and freshness of our ingredients and our friendly and fast service, we please our guests over and over again. The legendary Pan Pizza made us famous but you can also enjoy brand new pizza creations in our restaurants, such as the thin-crust Italian Style, the Cheezy Crust with delicious cheese baked in the crust and tasty pasta dishes and salads.

The most important part is we have knowledge and lessons learnt from mistakes we have done earlier. This is what makes us unique! Today, we are aiming up for the BIGGEST PIZZA in the world and looking forward to the challenge ahead!
